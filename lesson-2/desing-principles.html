Alignment is a design tool and refers to how elements are placed on a page. Form a visual appeal. Promotional texts, decorative or smaller texts, the other types are used strategically. Typically, the alignment applies to two specific design elements: typography and images. Graphic designers try to create a balance between the two so that both images and text are visually attractive and have a perfect connection. Through good use of alignment, a composition will appear orderly, clean and readable for an audience.

Balance

Balance refers to the fact that there is not too much weight either on one side or the other in both graphics and texts. The elements of art have weight visual weight. Balanced design with a balance to the eye of the beholder.
