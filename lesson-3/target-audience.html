Target Audience

The Site Weather Wind Vane should inform the weather forecast and provide clothing suggestions. Different users can access the site from a smartphone, iPad or computer.

Persona 1 - Valerie

Valerie Sofia Vergara is my daughter, she is 19 years old and has just finished the mission in California. He likes nature and every morning he goes for a walk. Soon you will go to BYU-I.

Scenario 1

Valerie wants to go to the mountains with her Peruvian friends Samantha and Melanie and wants to know what clothes to wear. Valerie will visit our SITE to know the weather conditions for the next days.

Scenario 2

Valerie needs to drive to visit her aunt who is in a nearby city. She worries that it rains at night because the tires of his car do not have a good grip in the rain. On our site Weather Wind Vane, she will find the information she needs.

Scenario 3

Valerie's brother will visit her this weekend. He wants to know if he wears a coat. On our site Weather Wind Vane, he will find the information he needs.

Scenario 4

Melanie Valerie's friend says that this weekend it will snow in Preston and that they will not be able to go to the concert of their favorite artists. On our site Weather Wind Vane, they will find the information they need.

Scenario 5

Valerie knows that there is a moon eclipse tonight. She wants to know a nearby place where she can visualize the eclipse. On our site Weather Wind Vane, she will find the information she needs.

Persona 2 - Daniel

Daniel Alejandro Vergara is my son and is 27 years old, married and has a 2-year-old son. He works at a city bank and attends college. He lives in Soda Spring, likes to play soccer and walks with his son. He shares his free time with his wife in cinemas, museums, and restaurants.

Scenario 1

Daniel Alejandro wants to know if tonight is a good opportunity to play soccer. Last week they couldn't play because it was raining. On our site Weather Wind Vane, he will find the information he needs.

Scenario 2

Daniel's wife asks him to help with dress Jacob his son. but he doesn't know the weather forecast to know what kind of clothes to wear. On the site Weather Wind Vane, he will find the information he needs.

Scenario 3

Daniel's car is quite dirty and he wants to wash it. His wife tells him not to waste time doing that task because it will rain in the afternoon. The site Weather Wind Vane will give him the information.

Scenario 4

Daniel Alejandro is planning a family barbecue this weekend. His brother-in-law tells him that it is not a good idea because there will be no sun. The site Weather Wind Vane will give him the information.

Scenario 5

Daniel Alejandro wants to go with his wife to dinner but she has the flu and she doesn't want to go because she says it's possible for it to rain tonight. What can they do?. The site Weather Wind Vane will give them the information.
