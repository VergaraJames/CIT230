Site Purpose

The purpose of the Weather Wind Vane is to provide a weather forecast information service. Initially, information on the climate of the places will be provided:

  • Preston
  • Soda Springs
  • Fish Haven
  • Idaho
This service will inform the user and travelers about the weather conditions and will give suggestions about the appropriate clothing, possible storms, among others.

Logo

The Weather Wind Vane Logo is represented by a rooster and at the bottom, there is an arrow that points the direction of the wind.
